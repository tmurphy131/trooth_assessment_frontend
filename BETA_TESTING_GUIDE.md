# T[root]H Discipleship - Beta Testing Guide

Thanks for testing! This app connects spiritual mentors with apprentices through biblical assessments, spiritual gifts discovery, and progress tracking.

**You'll need:** Two email addresses (one for Mentor account, one for Apprentice account) and about 30-45 minutes.

---

## PART 1: CREATE YOUR ACCOUNTS

### Create MENTOR Account
1. Open the app → tap **"Sign Up"**
2. Enter your first email address, create a password, tap **"Create Account"**
3. Select **"Mentor"** role → complete your profile with name and optional details
4. You'll see the Mentor Dashboard with your apprentice list (empty for now)
5. **Sign out**: Tap profile icon (top right) → scroll down → tap "Sign Out"

### Create APPRENTICE Account
1. Tap **"Sign Up"** → enter second email address, create password
2. Select **"Apprentice"** role → complete profile with your name
3. You'll see Apprentice Dashboard with action cards
4. Stay signed in as Apprentice for now

---

## PART 2: CONNECT MENTOR & APPRENTICE

### Mentor Sends Invitation
1. Sign out of Apprentice → sign in as **Mentor**
2. Tap **mail icon** (top right) → "Invite Apprentice"
3. Enter Apprentice's email and name → tap **"Send Invitation"**

### Apprentice Accepts
1. Sign out → sign in as **Apprentice**
2. Tap **people icon** (top right) → see pending invitation
3. Tap invitation → tap **"Accept"**

---

## PART 3: MENTORSHIP AGREEMENT

### Mentor Creates Agreement
1. Sign in as **Mentor**
2. Tap **document icon** (top right) → "Create Agreement"
3. Select your Apprentice → review terms → **sign** → "Send to Apprentice"

### Apprentice Signs
1. Sign in as **Apprentice**
2. Tap **document icon** → see pending agreement
3. Review terms → **sign** → "Submit"

---

## PART 4: TAKE ASSESSMENTS (as Apprentice)

### Master Trooth Assessment
1. On Dashboard → tap **"New Assessment"** card → select "Master Trooth Assessment"
2. Answer all questions thoughtfully (mix of multiple choice and open-ended)
3. Use the progress bar at top to track your completion
4. Tap **"Submit"** when finished → wait for AI scoring (10-30 seconds)
5. View your personalized results with scores by category and recommendations

### Spiritual Gifts Assessment
1. Return to Dashboard → tap **"Spiritual Gifts"** card
2. Read the disclaimer about spiritual gifts → tap "Continue"
3. Answer all questions honestly → Submit
4. See your top spiritual gifts with descriptions → tap "View Full Report" for details

### View Your Progress
1. Dashboard → tap **"View Progress"** card
2. See your assessment history, scores, and growth trends
3. Tap any past assessment to view detailed results again

---

## PART 5: MENTOR REVIEWS RESULTS

1. Sign in as **Mentor**
2. Tap **Apprentice's name** on Dashboard
3. Tap any assessment to view full report:
   - Overall score and category breakdown
   - AI recommendations for growth
   - Apprentice's answers
4. Add private **Notes** if desired (toggle "Share with Apprentice" to make visible)

---

## PART 6: EXPLORE MORE

- **Resources**: Both roles have guides and learning materials on Dashboard
- **Profile**: Tap profile icon to view/edit your information
- **Support**: Profile → scroll to "Need Help?" for support form

---

## REPORT FEEDBACK

**What to look for:**
- Account creation and sign-in works smoothly
- Mentor-apprentice invitation and connection flow
- Agreement signing process for both parties
- Assessment completion, AI scoring, and results display
- Mentor can view apprentice's results and reports
- Any crashes, errors, or confusing interface elements

**How to report:**
- Screenshot in TestFlight → sends feedback automatically
- Email **support@onlyblv.com** with description and screenshots
- Include: what you did, what happened, what you expected
